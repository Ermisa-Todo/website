My Journey

From management to graphic and web design.

My career began in customer service and then as an assistant manager where I developed a deep understanding of what it takes to build strong relationships and deliver exceptional service. During five years, I worked directly with clients and the team ensuring their needs were met, managing their expectations and delivering results that exceed their goals. This experience sharpened my communication, organization and project management skills, which have proved invaluable in my transition into graphic design. I shifted my focus to graphic design and web design and it has been an incredibly rewarding journey. I decided to follow my passion for visual storytelling and creativity. With a keen eye for design and understanding what works from my service background I have been privileged to work projects that combine artistic innovation with strategic purpose.

The Way I Work

Step 1: Reach Out

Start by sending me a message or filling out the contact form. I'll respond with some quick questions to get to know your project and goals.

Step 2: Project Proposal

I'll create a custom proposal with 2-3 package options (bronze, silver, gold), including timeline, deliverables, and pricing.

Step 3: Booking & Onboarding

You'll receive a contract, brand questionnaire, and a 50% deposit invoice.

Step 4: Design Phase

Here's where the magic happens! I'll design based on your input, with revisions to perfect the look and feel of your brand or site.

Step 5: Final Approval

Once approved, I'll send the final invoice. After payment, your full design package is prepared for delivery.

Step 6: Delivery

You'll receive everything in an organized folder — ready to launch and use across all platforms!

Step 7: Launch & Beyond

You're ready to shine! If you ever need support, social templates, or extra designs, I’m here for ongoing help.
