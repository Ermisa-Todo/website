My Journey

From management to graphic and web design.

My career began in customer service and then as an assistant manager where I developed a deep understanding of what it takes to build strong relationships and deliver exceptional service. During five years, I worked directly with clients and the team ensuring their needs were met, managing their expectations and delivering results that exceed their goals. This experience sharpened my communication, organization and project management skills, which have proved invaluable in my transition into graphic design. A year ago, I shifted my focus to graphic design and web design and it has been an incredibly rewarding journey. I decided to follow my passion for visual storytelling and creativity. With a keen eye for design and understanding what works from my service background I have been privileged to work projects that combine artistic innovation with strategic purpose.

Why Graphic & Web Design ?

  • Constant Learning & Growth:

    The design world is ever-changing, and I find that incredibly exciting. Each new trend or technique is a chance to learn something fresh and push my creative boundaries further.

  • Visual Storytelling:

    I love the power of design to tell a story, evoke emotions, and leave a lasting impact. Whether it's a website, a logo, or a branding project, I aim to create visuals that connect with people.

  • Freedom to Create & Innovate:

    Design is a field where I can continuously experiment, adapt, and push my creativity beyond limits. No two projects are the same, and that excites me!
